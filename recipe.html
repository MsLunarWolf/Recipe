Mermaid Grandma's Homemade Chicken Soup:

This soup is just the way grandma makes it. It is simple to make, and is super delicious!







Ingredients:

You will need:

(cross off/click when you have the item)
  1. 1 whole chicken skeleton
  2. 4 liters: water
  3. 3 sticks: celery
  4. 1 sprig: parsley
  5. 2 tsp.: whole pepper balls
  6. 2 bouleon cubes

How to Prepare:

Fill a large pot with 4 liters of water. Add all other ingredients to a pot and slowly bring to a boil. Let the soup boil for 30 minutes, then turn down the heat to a simmer, and let it simmer for 1 hour. Serve with rice or noodles. Garnish as desired.

To make a tomato soup

Simply add the following ingredients to your finished chicken soup:

  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of cream/coconut milk
